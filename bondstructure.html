Bond structure

Goldman Sachs underwrote three separate bond issuances totalling $6.5 billion (principal only) on behalf of 1MDB in a nine month period. Almost none of those funds produced any value to the Malaysian people. Most was stolen outright.

This section explains the bond deals as a foundation to understanding all other aspects of the case, including diversion of funds and massive governance failures.
